Sponsor packages

Why sponsor Drupalaton? 

Drupalaton is the official DrupalCamp in Hungary, being organized for the third time in Keszthely, and for the second time it's a 100% international event. 
Having workshops and trainings in English, we open the doors for everyone interested in Drupal. This year's goal is to broaden the knowledge of the attendees in different fields, deepen connections among people already using Drupal, provide personal and professional networking opportunities and also resources on how to use Drupal in general. During these days we expect to have more than 100 attendees to talk about front-end, business and anything that is related to Drupal.

Drupalaton is organized at Keszthely, a pretty and cosy lakeside town located on the western shore of Balaton, the biggest warm-water lake in Europe. This is the perfect opportunity to connect work and holiday!
